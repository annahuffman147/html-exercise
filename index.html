Where have I lived?

I was born in Oakland, California and my family moved to Grand Rapids, Michigan when I was 6 months old. My mom was a single mom of four children under 6 years old as she returned to college. Upon graduation, she moved us to Fremont, Michigan where she got her first job. I went to Michigan State University in East Lansing and moved to Detroit after graduation to volunteer in a group home. Two years later, I moved to Brownsburg, Indiana to support my sister who had left her abusive husband.....
